Three members of a family in Tashkent died from poisoning; possibly from gas

Parents and their three daughters were found unconscious in an apartment in the Koksaroy neighborhood of the Uchtepa district. According to the Kun.uz reporter, the incident happened on January 16, 2024. A 36-year-old mother and her 3-year-old daughter were found dead when the scene and corpses were examined by the investigative team. Also, the 39-year-old father, who was taken to the toxicology department of the Republican Emergency Medical Center in a critical condition, died on January 17 despite the medical assistance provided. The family's 12-year-old and 8-month-old daughters were admitted to the toxicology resuscitation department of the Republican Emergency Medical Center. They are also said to be in critical condition.
